Origins

Mythic Inspiration. Modern Precision.

Oepicus designs and manufactures expressive, professional-grade gear for modern musicians. Founded by experienced designers and engineers with over 30 years of combined expertise, Oepicus blends time-tested analog circuitry with advanced control features—bringing high-end synthesis concepts, including CV integration, to guitarists, synth players, and modular artists at a more accessible level.

These are not straight-up guitar pedals. Oepicus instruments invite focus, intention, and a genuine passion for sound, rewarding those who engage deeply with their craft. Built using professional assembly and in-house SMT manufacturing, every unit is produced under controlled conditions and tested to ensure zero variation and long-term reliability.

Inspired by music, timeless mythology, and epic stories—and guided by precision engineering—Oepicus builds instruments designed to endure on stage, in the studio, and across evolving creative journeys.
